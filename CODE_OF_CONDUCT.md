# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Giving and receiving feedback professionally
- Focusing on what is best for the community and project

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without permission
- Any conduct that is inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior. They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project.
